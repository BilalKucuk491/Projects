
Feature of Solent

- Corals, hydras, and seaflowers are fixed, and jellyfish are examples of swimmer secretaries.

-The substance exchange with water is done through the body surface.

Some species of secretaries live mutualist with other living things. For example, there is this association between sea anemone and clown fish.

Whip-like extensions called tentacles around the mouth openings are used for hunting and defense.

- In the animal kingdom, the first nervous system is found in the hydras from the secretaries.

-There are no specialized structures for respiration and excretion.

Sexual and asexual reproduction is seen.

They have radiative symmetry.
