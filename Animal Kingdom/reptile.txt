
Features of Reptiles

They live on land, in hot or regions with a mild climate. Their bodies are covered with scales made of keratin. They make lung breathing. They are cold-blooded creatures and hibernate.

Their hearts have three rooms. There is a half curtain in the turret section. However, the heart of crocodiles comes in four parts.

They reproduce sexually. Internal fertilization and external development are observed. Its eggs are covered with crust.

Snake, crocodile, lizard, dinosaur, turtle are the animals studied in this group.
